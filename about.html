About

A countdown timer. Why? Well it started one day in math class. I was really bored, so I timed the difference
between real time and school time. Then I got the idea of making a countdown timer. It was just some text
that showed you when school ended. Real simple. Then people liked the idea, so I improved it.
Except I made a terrible decision to just smack code onto the simple version so the code was really ugly
and inefficent. Well that continued for quite a while and you got all these random countdown timer versions.

The moral? Plan out your code. Who reads the about section anyways? It's a wall of text.
